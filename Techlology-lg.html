TechDay Festival

FPT Technology Forum (FPT Techday 2019) on November 21 demonstrated many 4.0 technology solutions such as virtual assistant, self-driving cars, and highly practical AI applications.

image of face recognition

FPT Techday 2019 took place on November 21 at the National Convention Center, attracting thousands of guests who are interested in technology and digital transformation. With the theme "Smart start", the festival showcased a series of products using artificial intelligence and demonstrated pioneering technological achievements of FPT.


Attendees at the event also tried to control the robot developed by students of FPT High School. This is a robot developed by you to participate in the 2018 World Robotics Competition and win a bronze medal in design. Robots have the ability to pick and move objects from one position to another. The development team hopes to develop this type of robot in the future to help people move furniture in difficult locations.

One of the most interesting things at FPT Techday 2019 is the experience of self-driving cars. The model developed by FPT and its Japanese partner can now drive at a maximum speed of 25km / h, avoid obstacles, brake to stop when there are unexpected obstacles, take turns and pick up passengers at reserved places. with absolute accuracy under test conditions.
